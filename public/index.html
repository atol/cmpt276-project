Welcome to DITU

Travel-Specific Platform

Ditu is a travel-specific social community platform that is a “one stop shop” for planning and sharing travel amongst friends and family.

Social Platform

users will be able to create profiles that show a variety of information regarding their travel experiences such as the number of countries or continents visited and will be able to add other users as “friends” who will be able to see each other’s profiles.
